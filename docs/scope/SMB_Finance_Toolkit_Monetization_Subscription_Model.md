# SMB Finance Toolkit

## Monetization Strategy -- Subscription Model

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Product Structure

Single application containing three financial modules:

-   **Profit / Margin Calculator**
-   **Break-even Calculator**
-   **Cashflow Forecaster**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Free Trial

-   Free trial period (duration to be defined).
-   Full access to all modules during trial.
-   No functional limitations during trial.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Subscription Model

After the trial period, users can subscribe on a monthly basis:

-   Individual subscription per module.
-   Bundle subscription including all three modules.
-   Bundle expected to be the primary monetization driver.
-   Pricing to be defined after MVP validation and scope stabilization.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Advertising Model

-   Single, small, non-intrusive banner block inside the application.
-   No interstitial ads.
-   No rewarded ads.
-   No auto-playing video.
-   Advertising remains present in all versions, including paid
    subscriptions.
-   Strict exclusion of gambling, betting, crypto scams, and misleading
    promotions.
-   Preference for business-relevant and productivity-related
    categories.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Strategic Positioning

-   Recurring SaaS-style monetization.
-   Functional value drives subscription decisions.
-   Advertising is a secondary revenue stream.
-   Long-term objective: flexible, modular product system.
